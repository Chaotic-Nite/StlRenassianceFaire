Welcome to Petite Lyon

King Franscois and King Henry the VIII grace the quiet area of Petite Lyon in Southern France during the Festival of the 1500s.
The village of Petite Lyon is over seen by the 13 Counts of the City, Lyon, and have seen fit to draw travelers, entertainers, peddlers and crafts masters from across the country to entertain those who come.
While the nobility visit who knows what intrigue will unfold.
While the realm of mortals toils over their Festive plans the Lord of the Forest holds his own court for revelries and a keen delight in startling those of mortal kind and inspiring wonder in those with an open mind.
Jousts, music, fine foods and wares, friends new and old, and cheer will appear.

Come and find what strikes you merry!
